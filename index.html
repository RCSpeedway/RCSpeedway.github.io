Rose City Speedway

Indoor carpet RC racing. Serving the Niagara Region and beyond.

Indoor RC Carpet Racing

Using CRC carpet, we offer a full time RC track for racing and practice. Both On Road and Oval racing is run on a weekly schedule on our 75' x 45' track.

All Skill Levels Welcome

Whether you're a novice or an expert driver, our track is available for you to sharpen your skills on the carpet. Classes include, but are not limited to: 1/12 Scale, World GT-R, Formula 1, GT12, USVTA, USGT, Touring Car.
